About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Developer

I specialize in developing high-performance, responsive websites and mobile applications that meet your business needs. With extensive experience in both front-end and back-end development, I deliver custom solutions—from a quick one-page design to comprehensive web applications. My expertise covers modern front-end technologies like HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, React.js, and Next.js. On the back-end, I work with Node.js and Express.js to build robust server-side applications. Additionally, I integrate advanced features such as Stripe payment processing, order tracking systems, and real-time chat functionalities to create seamless and efficient user experiences.
